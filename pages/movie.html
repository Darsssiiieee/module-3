Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is a 2016 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is the 14th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film was directed by Scott Derrickson and stars Benedict Cumberbatch as neurosurgeon Dr. Stephen Strange along with a star-studded cast. After a horrific car accident, Strange embarks on a journey of healing where he encounters the Ancient One and learns about the mystic arts.

Avengers Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is the direct sequel to Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and the 22nd film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film features an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy Renner, and others. The Avengers join forces with allies to reverse the damage caused by Thanos in Infinity War.

Avengers Infinity War

Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers. Produced by Marvel Studios and distributed by Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures, it is the sequel to 2012's The Avengers and 2015's Avengers: Age of Ultron, and the 19th film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The film features an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, and others. The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.

Taylor Swift's Eras Tour

Taylor Swift's Eras Tour is a captivating musical journey that showcases the evolution of Swift's music across different eras. This concert film features performances of her greatest hits, behind-the-scenes footage, and exclusive interviews, providing an intimate look at the iconic artist's creative process.

Snowdrop

Snowdrop is a South Korean television series that takes place in 1987. It tells the love story of a student at a prestigious university who, against all odds, falls for a North Korean spy. The drama is filled with suspense, romance, and historical context, making it a must-watch for K-drama fans.

Bigmouth

Bigmouth is a thrilling South Korean crime drama series. The story revolves around a seemingly ordinary high school teacher who becomes entangled in the underworld of organized crime. This gripping series is filled with unexpected twists and turns, making it a must-watch for fans of intense, character-driven narratives.
